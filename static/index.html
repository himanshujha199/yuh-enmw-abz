How It Works

Three steps.
One phone call.

No app download. No internet. No literacy required. Just a phone call in your language.

01
📞

Call & Speak

Dial the Gram Sahayak number and speak naturally in Hindi, Tamil, or Telugu. Describe your situation — the AI understands context, not just keywords.

02
🔍

Instant Matching

Our semantic engine searches across government schemes, health protocols, and emergency services to find exactly what's relevant to your profile.

03
💡

Guided Action

Get step-by-step guidance in your language — which documents to bring, where to apply, what to do next. The agent remembers you for follow-ups.
